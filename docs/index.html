For the Kids

Our analytics will help children understand their disease, learn about their treatment, take their medications, forecast their health, and improve their health after treatment.

For the Doctors

Our analytics will help doctors characterize childhood cancer, prioritize their treatment, predict their response and track their recovery.

The Childhood Cancer Directory

Join

The Childhood Cancer Directory is a support network for kids experiencing cancer. We list valuable resources for kids with cancer and their families. We have mentors, including cancer survivors and medical students, who meet with them and help in all sorts of ways. We want to not only get them through cancer, but make them successful in life.
