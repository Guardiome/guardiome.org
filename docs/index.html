Guardiome

We are building health analytics for childhood cancer – so that doctors can prescribe informed treatment and children can engage with their treatment.

For the Kids

Our analytics will help children understand their disease, learn about their treatment, take their medications, forecast their health, and maximize their well-being after treatment.

For the Doctors

Our analytics will help doctors characterize childhood cancer, prioritize treatment, predict response, and track recovery.

The Childhood Cancer Directory

Join

The Childhood Cancer Directory is a support network for kids experiencing cancer. Its our way of connecting directly with childhood cancer patients to support them and listen to them. We have mentors, including cancer survivors and medical students, who meet with them weekly and help in all sorts of ways. If you are a childhood cancer patient, or want to help, sign up for the directory here.
